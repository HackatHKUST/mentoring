Before PennApps

Code Weekend

PennApps has teamed up with The Dining Philosophers to teach new hackers how to make web apps. Code Weekend was two days of learning on Saturday September 6 and Sunday September 7 in the Penn Engineering quad.

There were four tutorials over the course of the weekend. The first was about the basics of the Internet, including HTML, CSS, Javascript, and how they interact on a web page. The second was an introduction to Node. Talk three went over how to integrate the Venmo API into a Node app, and the fourth was about MongoDB integration.

Click here to see all the information from the tutorials, including slides, code samples (split into milestones), and essentially a transcript from the first session!

During PennApps

Kick-off Event

Dedicated to coming to PennApps, but not sure where to start? Still looking for a team? Fear not! This session will start of with a quick introduction to coming up with a successful idea and picking great teammates. Most of the time will be spent in team formation, or meeting other programmers looking to form teams. There will be tons of mentors present to help you out as well!


The event will be at Wu and Chen Auditorium, immediately following dinner on Friday.
